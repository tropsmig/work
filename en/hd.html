The basic version is an app based on schedule management and groupware, featuring highly useful functions for daily life such as to-do lists, voting, and cards. The currently released premium version includes HODUWORKS (electronic authorization), HODUDOC (hospital appointments), and HODUHOME (visitor management, real estate listings, and second-hand transactions).

This app involved the redesign of an existing schedule management app, along with the planning, design, and front-end development of additional features, all carried out simultaneously.

Calendar
Weekdays, including Saturday, are displayed in black text, while Sunday is highlighted in red text to distinguish the starting day of the week. The lunar calendar is added below the Sunday date in gray font, and today's date is marked with a circle in the main theme color and white text. Public holidays are indicated with red text on a light pink background, all-day events are distinguished by a background color matching the selected event's color and white text, and scheduled events with specific times are separated by a colored bar next to the event title.

calendar app image

schedule app image

Add Schedule
Event registration is divided into personal and group schedules, and users can select which type to register when adding an event (group events are registered under the group schedule). The time selection design for events places the start time on the left and the end time on the right, providing an intuitive layout. All close and save buttons within the app are positioned at the top for easy access.

schedule app image

Lists of Schedules
Without the need to click on each event, we’ve displayed as much information as possible for each schedule. Multiple events on the same date are listed together without overlap, and a thick gray line is used to visually separate events by date. Tasks with deadlines on the same date are placed below the event list for clarity.

list of schedules app image

schedule and todo app image

To-Do & Survey
Tasks are divided into three sections: All, In Progress, and Completed, with the total count displayed on the right side of the tab titles. For polls, we designed the interface to include the profile image of the creator at the top, the poll’s expiration date, and additional details such as options for multiple votes or anonymous voting.

Invite
The previously complex card feature has been simplified as much as possible in both planning and design, with the addition of the Blue Card design. Users can create custom cards using their own photos. This feature is highly versatile and can be used for party invitations, wedding invitations, and other occasions.

Chat
For user convenience, the design closely mirrors that of many popular chat apps. The chat type is distinguished by the number of user profile images or the group image, making it easy to differentiate between individual and group chats.
