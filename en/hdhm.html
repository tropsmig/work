HODU's premium version, HODUHOME, allows users to manage visitors, discover nearby locations, and buy, sell, or share second-hand products. It is primarily designed for users living in apartment complexes, and the main color for this version is purple.

This version is a project that followed the redesign of the basic version and the HODUDOC design. The planning and design were done with the overall design already established.

Visitors
This feature is designed for apartment residents to reserve parking by adding visitors' car numbers in advance. After registration, approval from the administrator is required. Requests are marked in yellow, completed in blue, and denied in red.
