JANDIK is an online shopping platform that offers essential products such as clothing and furniture, carefully curated by professional designers. The site aims to ease the burdens of modern life by providing high-end products for users who are looking to invest in quality. It targets consumers seeking premium items, offering a streamlined and refined shopping experience.

The design combines bold lines with a contrasting simple white background to create a clean and minimalist look. Disruptive elements are minimized to ensure the focus remains solely on the products.

Main

The overall design flow is established by using promotional images of high-end products. On the main content section, the left side features product details, while the right side showcases product images.

  • Layout

    The header features the logo and main menu, while the main content layout has product details on the left and brand-related submenus and product images on the right. The footer includes subscription and customer service (CS) sections. The header and footer are consistent across all screens.
