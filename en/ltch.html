LITCH is an app that integrates music with movies and series, available in two versions: a standard user version and a creator version. The professional version, designed for artists and content creators, provides an opportunity to showcase their work, while the general user version allows users to enjoy music and content through LITCH.

The app was conceived and designed to address the inconvenience of using multiple music and content apps. Bold, rounded borders were incorporated into the design to add vibrancy and a sense of approachability throughout the interface.

Watch

As an OTT platform, LITCH allows users to search for and watch their desired movies and series. Additionally, users can view content uploaded by creators through the app.

  • Watch the content you want through the Watch section

    The main color, purple, symbolizes the night sky, inspired by the image of unwinding with a movie after a long day. It represents the hope for a peaceful end to the day.

    On the main area, featured content is displayed, and the play button on the tab bar now shows the most recently viewed content, with its image as the background.

Content Detail

  • The Content Detail for movies and series.

    After researching various OTT platforms, I designed a familiar, user-friendly interface with a clear structure: Content Type > Watch Button > Essential Info > Movie Details. Tabs for Details, Reviews, Recommended Content, and Episodes (for series) are included.

    The review section is placed next to the content name for easy access, allowing users to view it without extra scrolling.
