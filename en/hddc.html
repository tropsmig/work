HODUDOC is the premium version of HODU, offering features like finding hospitals, making appointments, and viewing medical status. After signing up through the basic version group tab, users can easily manage their appointments. Frequently visited hospitals can be saved, and users can track their medical history and next appointments by date.

This app was planned and designed after the third redesign of the basic version. The overall design follows the same structure as the basic version, with teal chosen as the main color to create a calming and soothing atmosphere.
