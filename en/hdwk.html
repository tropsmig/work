HODUWORKS, the premium version of HODU, is packed with essential business features such as electronic authorization, attendance management, task tracking, and employee management. Designed for companies of all sizes, from SMEs to large enterprises, it simplifies approval processes and document storage in the digital age. This is the latest premium version I developed for HODU.

HODUWORKS is a completely redesigned version of the previous HODU CORPORATION (another premium version), excluding its business functions. It has two versions: one for administrators and one for employees. I did not participate in the publishing phase. (The HODUWORKS logo in the image above is a temporary text addition.)

Electronic Authorization
The approval process is divided into 4 steps: Progress (green), Rejected (red), Completed (blue), and Pending (gray). The electronic approval list uses progress bars with corresponding colors for intuitive design.
